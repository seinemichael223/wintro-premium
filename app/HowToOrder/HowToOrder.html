    HOW TO ORDER

    STEP 1: Select A Product

    Browse through our wide range of products, including trophies, medals, custom gifts, and more. Click on the product you wish to purchase to view detailed information.

    STEP 2: Personalize Your Product

    Add a personal touch by entering the required customization details, such as:

    1. Text: Names, dates, or any special message.

    2. Logo: Upload your organization or event logo.

    3. Special Instructions: Mention specific requests, such as font styles or placement preferences.

    STEP 3: Make an Online Payment

    Choose a convenient payment method:

    • Credit/Debit Card
    • Online Banking

    Ensure your payment details are accurate before proceeding.

    STEP 4: Track Your Order

    Once your order is shipped, you'll receive an email notification containing the tracking number. Use the tracking number to monitor your parcel's delivery status.
